HUGH GORDON ARCHITECT

Problem

Hugh Gordon Architect is a leading Canberra architect, and required a new web presence. Their previous site, while containing all the right information, was visually outdated and hard to navigate.

Content was not easily modified - Despite a rapidly growing portfolio. Hugh Gordon Architect required a continuous stream of updates to their site.

Their search engine standings were also poor - showing on the second page for relevant keywords.

Solution

A modern, Content Management System-based website was designed and implemented - allowing Hugh Gordon Architect to log into and manage their own content.

Fully mobile friendly design. Over 51.3% of users view websites through a mobile device. Capturing this market is essential.

The new website is fully search engine optimised. Resulting in a jump of over seven Google rankings, and increase in traffic of over 140%.
