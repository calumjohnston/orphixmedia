CASE STUDY

HUGH GORDON ARCHITECT

Problem

Hugh Gordon Architect is a leading Canberra architecture firm, and required a new web presence. Their previous site, while containing all the right information, was visually outdated and hard to navigate.

Content was not easily modified - Despite a rapidly growing portfolio. Hugh Gordon Architect required a continuous stream of updates to their site.

Their search engine standings were also poor - showing on the second page for relevant keywords.

Solution

A modern, CMS-based website was designed and implemented - allowing Hugh Gordon Architect staff to log into their own website, and manage their own content.

A fully mobile friendly design. Over 51% of internet users view websites through a mobile device - Capturing this market is essential.

The new website is search engine optimised. Resulting in a jump of over seven Google rankings. We're pleased to hear that HGA have seen a sharp increase in traffic and as a result, clients.
